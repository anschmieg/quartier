Research Methodology

Last edited 2 hours ago · 342 words

This document outlines our reproducible research workflow using Quarto. The approach integrates version control directly into the writing process.

By treating research documents as code, we enable collaborative iteration with full history tracking. Each change is atomic, reviewable, and reversible.

The methodology emphasizes clarity over complexity. Simple workflows that teams can actually follow beat elaborate systems that sit unused.
