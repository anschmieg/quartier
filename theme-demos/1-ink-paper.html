On the Nature of Scientific Inquiry

Draft — Last edited December 11, 2025

Research is the systematic investigation into materials and sources in order to establish facts and reach new conclusions. This document explores the fundamental principles that guide modern scientific methodology.

The pursuit of knowledge has been a defining characteristic of human civilization. From the ancient philosophers of Greece to the modern laboratories of today, we have sought to understand the world around us.

In this work, we present a comprehensive framework for conducting reproducible research using Quarto documents.
